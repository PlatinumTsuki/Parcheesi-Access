# Credits

Parcheesi Access uses a number of third-party assets, all of which are either in the public domain or distributed under permissive licenses. This file lists every asset, its source, and any attribution required.

The compositions of every musical piece used are in the **public domain** worldwide (all composers died more than a century ago). Where attribution is required, it is for the **recording / performance / arrangement**, not the composition itself.

## Game sound effects

The 40 short sound effects used during gameplay (dice, piece selection, captures, victory stings, timer ticks, pause/resume, urgency bells, navigation, etc.) come from sound asset packs by **Kenney Vleugels (Kenney.nl)**, all released under [**Creative Commons Zero (CC0)**](http://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) — public domain, no attribution required.

- Source: [https://kenney.nl/assets](https://kenney.nl/assets)
- Although CC0 attribution is not mandatory, we are pleased to credit Kenney for their generous contribution to the indie game community.
